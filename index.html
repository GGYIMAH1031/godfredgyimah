ABOUT ME...

“The future is ours to shape. I feel we are in a race that we need to win. It’s a race between the growing power of the technology and the growing wisdom we need to manage it.” ~ Max Tegmark

I completed college with a degree in Civil Engineering from the Kwame Nkrumah University of Science & Technology (KNUST). After graduation, I worked for three years at Huawei Technologies as an engineering analyst, focusing on customer analytics. The next stop in my journey was a Masters in Engineering program at University of Leeds (United Kingdom), where I specialized in numerical modeling. Following my graduation in August 2014, I started a PhD in Mining Engineering at Missouri University of Science & Technology under the guidance of Prof. Samuel Frimpong. My PhD research was in the area of Applied Machine Learning and Numerical Modeling. I defended my PhD dissertation in June 2018. In January 2018, I joined Bayer Crop Science as a research data scientist under their leadership program, Emerging Leaders in Science (ELS).
